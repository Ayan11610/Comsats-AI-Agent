# Admissions – COMSATS University Islamabad (CUI)

## Overview
COMSATS University Islamabad (CUI) conducts admissions for Undergraduate (BS), Graduate (MS/MPhil), and Doctoral (PhD) programs through a centralized online admission system. Admissions are offered across multiple campuses based on merit.

Official Admissions Portal:
https://admissions.comsats.edu.pk/

---

## 1. Admission Cycles
CUI typically offers admissions in the following cycles:

- **Fall Admissions** (Major intake – all programs)
- **Spring Admissions** (Limited programs, mainly graduate level)

Admission announcements are published on the official website and campus portals.

Admissions are available for:

### Undergraduate Programs
- BS Computer Science
- BS Software Engineering
- BS Electrical Engineering
- BS Business Administration
- BS Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, etc.

### Graduate Programs
- MS / MPhil programs in various disciplines

### Doctoral Programs
- PhD programs (research-based)

Programs vary by campus.

## 3. Creating an Admission Account (Sign-Up)

To apply for admission, students must create an account on the admission portal.

### Steps to Create an Account
1. Visit: https://admissions.comsats.edu.pk/
2. Click on **“Register / Sign Up”**
3. Provide:
   - Full Name
   - CNIC / B-Form
   - Email Address
   - Mobile Number
4. Create a password
5. Verify email (if required)
6. Account is successfully created

Each applicant can create **only one account** using a unique CNIC.

## 4. Login Procedure

### How to Login
1. Go to: https://admissions.comsats.edu.pk/
2. Click **Login**
3. Enter:
   - CNIC
   - Password
4. Access your applicant dashboard

### Dashboard Features
- Application form

## 5. Admission Application Process

## 7. Entry Test Options

Applicants can apply using **either**:

### Option 1: COMSATS Entry Test
- Conducted by CUI
- Campus-based
- Marks used directly in merit calculation

### Option 2: NTS NAT (National Aptitude Test)
- Conducted by National Testing Service (NTS)
- Accepted by COMSATS for admissions

---

## 8. NTS NAT (National Aptitude Test)

Official Website:
https://nts.org.pk/new/NAT.php

### What is NTS NAT?
NAT is a standardized test conducted by NTS for admission into universities across Pakistan.

### Types of NAT
- **NAT-IE** – Engineering
- **NAT-ICS** – Computer Science
- **NAT-IM** – Management Sciences
- **NAT-IA** – Arts & Social Sciences
- **NAT-IG** – General

### NAT Validity
- NAT result is valid for **1 year**
- Applicants can improve scores by retaking the test

### NAT Marks Usage
- Total marks: 100
- Used as **entry test component** in merit calculation

## 9. Merit Criteria & Weightage

### Undergraduate Merit Formula (Typical)
- Matric: **10%**
- Intermediate: **40%**
- Entry Test (NTS / CUI): **50%**

*(Exact weightage may vary slightly by program)*

---

## 10. Aggregate Calculation (Merit)

### Aggregate Formula

- Aggregate =
- (Matrc % × 0.10) +
- (Intermediate % × 0.40) +
- (Entry Test % × 0.50)


### Example Calculation
- Matric: 85%
- Intermediate: 80%
- Entry Test: 75%

- Aggregate =
- (85 × 0.10) +
- (80 × 0.40) +
- (75 × 0.50)

- = 8.5 + 32 + 37.5
- = 78%


Higher aggregate = higher chance of admission.

5. Receive roll number / registration details

Failure to submit fee results in **seat cancellation**.
