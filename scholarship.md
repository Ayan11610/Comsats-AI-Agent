# COMSATS University Scholarships & Financial Aid

COMSATS University provides a variety of scholarships and financial support programs to promote educational access and excellence. These include merit-cum-need-based scholarships, external scholarships, financial assistance programs, and other special funds.

## Overview

- **Total Internal Scholarships (FY 2024-25):** Rs. 1,057 Million for 15,611 students  
- **Total External Scholarships (FY 2024-25):** Rs. 307 Million for 1,838 students  
- **Total Scholarship Disbursement:** Rs. 1,364 Million for 17,449 students  

These scholarships cover tuition fees and provide additional opportunities for academic and personal growth.

## Internal Scholarships / CUI Financial Aid Program

- Applicable for BS, MS, and PhD students
- Merit-cum-Need-based
- Financial support to ensure students are not hindered by financial constraints

## External Scholarships

- Supported through partnerships with donor agencies
- Available for BS, MS, and PhD students
- Aims to empower students to excel academically and professionally

## Special Financial Support Programs

- **Taleem Fund**: Targeted support for deserving students  
- **Qarz-i-Hasna**: Interest-free loans for students  
- **Kinship / Siblings Concession**: Fee concession for siblings enrolled simultaneously  
- **Academic Excellence Incentives**: Scholarships based on academic performance

These scholarships are administered through donor agencies while coordination is done via the Student Financial Aid Office (SFAO) at respective campuses.
