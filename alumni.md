## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

### What is the CUI Alumni Portal?

### Who can register?
All graduates of COMSATS University Islamabad from **all campuses and programs**.

By visiting the alumni portal and completing the registration form using graduation details and student credentials.

Yes. Alumni can update personal, academic, and professional information through their profile dashboard.
