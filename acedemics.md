# Academics – COMSATS University Islamabad (CUI)

## Overview
COMSATS University Islamabad (CUI) offers a comprehensive academic system across multiple physical campuses and a Virtual Campus. The university provides Undergraduate (BS), Graduate (MS/MPhil), and Doctoral (PhD) programs in science, engineering, management, humanities, and emerging technologies.

Official Academic Programs Page:
https://www.comsats.edu.pk/academic-programs.aspx

---

## 1. Academic Structure

### Degree Levels
- Undergraduate (BS)
- Graduate (MS / MPhil)
- Doctoral (PhD)

### Academic System
- Semester-based system
- Credit hour structure
- Continuous assessment (quizzes, assignments, mid, final)
- Course outlines and results managed via CUOnline
